Beryl Hovis-Afflerbach

I am a third year undergraduate student majoring in astrophysics at Caltech in Pasadena, California.


I am currently working at Carnegie Observatories, studying massive stars stripped in binaries with Dr. Ylva Götberg.


My previous research includes solar eruptions and space weather, tracking polar faculae on the sun, and dimensionality reduction for galaxy redshift determination.
