About Me

I am a third year undergraduate student majoring in astrophysics at Caltech in Pasadena, California.


My previous research includes solar eruptions and space weather, the evolution of massive binary stars, and dimensionality reduction for galaxy redshift determination.


I am currently working in the Heliophysics Science Division at NASA Goddard Space Flight Center, studying the evolution of solar prominence eruptions with Dr. Barbara Thompson and polar faculae with Dr. Dean Pesnell.
