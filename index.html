About Me

Beryl Hovis-Afflerbach

(they/she)


I am a fourth year undergraduate student majoring in astrophysics at Caltech in Pasadena, California.


I am currently working with Jim Fuller and Shing Chi Leung at Caltech, modeling the silicon burning phase for 8-11 solar mass stars.


My previous research includes modeling populations of stars stripped in binaries at varying metallicity, solar eruptions and space weather, tracking polar faculae on the sun, and dimensionality reduction for galaxy redshift determination.
